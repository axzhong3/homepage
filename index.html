• Andrew Zhong

Andrew is an aspiring computer vision and software engineer, with experience tackling real-time computer vision challenges in augmented reality, social gaming and autonomous driving. He contributed to perception stack for Magic Leap One, 3d AI-designed avatars for IMVU, IPhone architecture design for Apple, and autonomous vehicle perception and planning for Lyft Level 5.

He graduated with a Master's degree in Computer Science (Visual Computing and Computer Graphics) at the University of California, Berkeley. He received his Bachelor's degree in ECE from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.
